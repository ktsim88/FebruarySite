Accomplishments

FDR went to many conferences with Churchill and Stallin. He went to Casablanca Conference with Churchill only. The plan was to invade Italy and make them surrender. He went to the Tehran Conference which was to liberate France out of German control. The plan also included invading Germany and go to war against Japan. The Yalta Conference was the plan after World War 2. Germany would be divided into different zones, Soviety Union would go to war against Japan. They would hold a conference to make an organization for world peace (United Nations)

Legacy & Impact

FDR died on April 12, 1945 in Little White House in Georgia from cerebal hemorrhage. He died in office. His presidency led to the 22nd Amendment which stated that a president cannot serve more than two terms. He created many laws and acts that are still in place to this day.
