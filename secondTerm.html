Second Term: Great Depression

He still used his New Deal campaign. He started to focus on foreign policy since WW2 broke out during his second term. The US was still in the Great Depression but it was improving.
Laws Passed

  • Court-packing Bill: reorganize the Supreme Court with the people he chooses (failed)
  • Fair Labor Standards Act: minimum wage of 40 cents and maximum work time of 40 hours per week. This also placed child labor restrictions to under 16
  • Selective Service Act: training men for WW2
There were also Neutrality Acts to prevent the US from getting involved with foreign policy.
    Neutrality Acts:
  • 1935: cannot ship guns and US citizens can’t travel on boats
  • 1936: forbid loans to other countries
  • 1937: forbid shipping guns to Spain during the Civil War
US started to break away from Neutrality and helped Great Britian
