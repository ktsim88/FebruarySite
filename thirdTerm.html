Third Term: World War 2

World War 2 broke out and the US was trying to stay neutral.
Laws Passed & Accomplishments

  • Lend-Lease Act: End neutrality and gave Britain guns when needed
  • Smith-Connally Anti-Strike Act of 1943: stopped strikes during the war
Accomplishments
US must defend the four freedoms: speech, religion, want, and fear. They tried to negotiate with Japan with oil but it failed. This led to the Attack on Pearl Harbor and US declared war a few days later.

US DECLARES WAR

The Attack on Pearl Harbor led to many people dead. FDR made this speech to let the Americans know that we are going to war.

Controversies

He did not have a lot of controversy besides being the first president to break the two terms and the debate about the war. Some feel that the US should have stayed neutral
